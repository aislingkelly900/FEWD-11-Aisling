Scrambled eggs

  • 2 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • milk
  1. crack the 2 eggs into a bowl
  2. beat the eggs and add in salt and pepper to your taste
  3. add in a small amount of milk, and beat with the eggs
  4. finally add in a knob of butter
  5. heat the pot, then pour the mixture into the pot
  6. keep stirring until fully cooked
  7. serve on fresh toast

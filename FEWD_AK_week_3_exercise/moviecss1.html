Braveheart

Braveheart is a 1995 American epic historical drama film directed and produced by Mel Gibson, who protrys Scottish warrior William Wallace in the first war of Scottish Independence against King Edward I of England. The main cast members include:

  • Mel Gibson as William Wallace
  • Sophie Marceau as Princess Isabelle
  • Patrick McGoohan as King Longshanks - King Edward I
  • Catherine McCormack as Murron MacClannough, Wallace's wife
  • Angus McFayden as Robert the Bruce
  • Brendan Gleeson as Hamish.
